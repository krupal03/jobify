Jobify

#Project Description :
	Jobify is Web-based Application in which Jobseeker can search for the job and can apply to their corresponding job.
Jobseeker can filled and Upload their Resume. Then Jobseeker get notified whenever Recruiter Change the 
status of their job request.
	Recruiter can Register themself and post new jobs as their company's requirement.

#Starting Information
	starting page : /Jobify/Home/Index
	Users : Admin, Recruiter, Jobseeker
